Resistance Training Guide

What is Resistance Training?

Resistance training is a form of exercise that improves strength and muscle size by working against a force. This can include weights, resistance bands, or even bodyweight exercises like push-ups and squats.

Benefits of Resistance Training

Resistance training helps build muscle, increase metabolism, and improve overall health. It also supports fat loss while maintaining lean muscle mass, making it one of the most effective training styles.

  • Builds muscle strength
  • Boosts metabolism
  • Improves bone density
  • Supports fat loss
